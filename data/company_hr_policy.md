# LexiHR Copilot — Policy & Benefits Guide (2026)

## 1. Paid Time Off (PTO) & Leave Policy

### 1.1 Privilege Leave (PL)
- **Annual Entitlement:** All full-time employees are entitled to **18 days** of Privilege Leave per calendar year.
- **Accrual Rate:** Leaves accrue at a rate of 1.5 days per month completed.
- **Carry Forward & Encashment:** A maximum of **10 days** of unused PL can be carried forward to the next calendar year. Any remaining balance above 10 days will be automatically encashed at the end of the financial year based on the employee's basic salary.
- **Approval Process:** Requests for PL exceeding 3 consecutive days must be submitted via the HR portal at least 7 working days in advance and require managerial approval.

### 1.2 Sick / Casual Leave (SL/CL)
- **Annual Entitlement:** Employees receive **12 days** of combined Sick and Casual Leave per year (allocated on January 1st).
- **Carry Forward:** Sick/Casual leaves **cannot** be carried forward or encashed. Unused SL/CL expires on December 31st each year.
- **Medical Certificate Requirement:** Any sick leave extending beyond **2 consecutive working days** mandates the submission of a registered medical practitioner's certificate upon return to work.

## 2. Remote Work & Hybrid Workplace Guidelines

### 2.1 The 3-2 Hybrid Model
- **Core Schedule:** Apex Technologies operates on a structured hybrid schedule. Employees are required to work from their designated regional office (e.g., Chennai, Bangalore) **3 days per week (Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday)**.
- **Remote Days:** Mondays and Fridays are designated as flexible remote-work days, provided all project deliverables and client SLAs are met.

### 2.2 Equipment & WFH Allowance
- **Hardware Allocation:** The company provides a primary laptop, dual monitors, and peripheral peripherals upon onboarding.
- **Monthly Stipend:** Employees receive a monthly WFH reimbursement allowance of **INR 1,500** to cover high-speed internet and ergonomic setup expenses. This is automatically credited with the monthly payroll.

## 3. Parental Leave & Benefits

### 3.1 Maternity Leave
- **Duration:** Female employees are entitled to **26 weeks** of fully paid maternity leave for up to two surviving children, in compliance with the Maternity Benefit Act.
- **Pre-natal Flexibility:** Up to 8 weeks of leave can be initiated prior to the expected date of delivery.

### 3.2 Paternity Leave
- **Duration:** Male employees are entitled to **10 working days** of fully paid paternity leave.
- **Utilization Window:** Paternity leave must be utilized within the first **3 months** of the child's birth or adoption.

## 4. Code of Conduct & Moonlighting Policy

### 4.1 Exclusivity of Service (Moonlighting)
- **Strict Prohibition:** While employed at Apex Technologies, employees are strictly prohibited from engaging in any secondary employment, freelance consulting, or advisory roles (paid or unpaid) without prior written authorization from the Chief Human Resources Officer (CHRO).
- **Consequences:** Unauthorized moonlighting constitutes a severe breach of trust and will result in immediate disciplinary review, up to and including termination of employment.
